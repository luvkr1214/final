About

Backstage – A platform where talent finds its stage,

Backstage is more than just an event—it’s an emotion, a dream, and a passion for countless talented individuals who haven't had the chance to shine. Whether it’s due to lack of opportunities or other barriers, many gifted people never get to showcase their skills. We are here to change that! Backstage is a platform where talent knows no limits—whether you sing, dance, walk the ramp, perform stand-up comedy, recite poetry, or express yourself in any other way. There are no age restrictions—anyone can step into the spotlight, discover their inner talent, and experience the joy of performing.


Why Join Backstage

Backstage is where talent shines and dreams come true. We offer a platform for all, no matter your skill or background. Step up, showcase your talent, and let your passion take center stage!
